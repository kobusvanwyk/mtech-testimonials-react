Step-by-step guide

How to Submit Your Testimonial

Follow these simple steps — it only takes a few minutes.

The 7 steps

1
Your Details
Enter your first and last name. If you prefer, tick the "Stay anonymous" box and your name will not be shown publicly.
2
Health Condition
Type the health condition(s) your story relates to. For more than one, separate them with a comma.
e.g. Diabetes, High Blood Pressure
3
Products Used
Select the Mannatech product(s) that your testimonial is about. Tap each one to select it.
4
Your Story
Write your testimonial in your own words. What was your health challenge? What changed? There is no right or wrong way — just be honest.
💡 Tip

You don't need to write an essay. Even a few sentences about what changed for you is perfect.

5
Photos (optional)
You may add photos if you like — for example before/after photos or product photos. This step is completely optional. Tap "Next" to skip it.
6
Consent
Tick the three checkboxes to confirm you agree to the Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy, and that you give us permission to use your story.
7
Review & Submit
Check that everything looks correct, then tap the big green "Submit" button. That's it — you're done!
