Step-by-step guide

How to Submit
Your Testimonial

Follow these 7 simple steps to share your Mannatech health story. It only takes a few minutes.

Mannatech
South Africa mtechtestimonials.co.za

Open the form on your phone or computer by visiting mtechtestimonials.co.za/submit — or scan the QR code below. Then follow the steps one by one. Use the Next button to move forward and the Back button if you need to change something.

1
Your Details
Enter your first and last name. If you prefer not to show your name publicly, tick the "Stay anonymous" box.
2
Health Condition
Type the health condition(s) your story relates to. For more than one, separate them with a comma.
e.g. Diabetes, High Blood Pressure
3
Products Used
Select the Mannatech product(s) your testimonial is about. Tap or click each one to select it. You can select more than one.
4
Your Story
Write your testimonial in your own words. What was your health challenge? What changed after using the product? There is no right or wrong — just be honest.
5
Photos (optional)
You may add photos if you like — for example before/after or product photos. This step is completely optional. Just tap "Next" to skip it.
6
Consent
Tick the three checkboxes to confirm you agree to the Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy, and that you give permission for your story to be used.
7
Review & Submit
A summary of everything you entered will appear. Check that it looks correct, then tap the big green "Submit" button. That's it — your testimonial has been sent for review. Thank you!
💡 Helpful tips

Stuck on a step? Use the Back button to go back and change something. Your progress is saved as you go.

Writing your story: You don't need to write an essay. Even just a few honest sentences about what changed for you is perfect and very valuable.

Anonymous option: If you tick "Stay anonymous" on step 1, your name will not be shown anywhere on the website.
