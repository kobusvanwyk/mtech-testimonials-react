💡 Helpful tips

Stuck on a step? Use the Back button to go back and change something. Your progress is saved as you go.

Writing your story: You don't need to write an essay. Even just a few honest sentences about what changed for you is perfect.

Anonymous: If you tick "Stay anonymous" on step 1, your name will not be shown anywhere on the website.
